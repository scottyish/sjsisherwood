About me

I’m a problem solver, investor, and passionate advocate for advancing life sciences and healthcare research. I hold a PhD in cognitive neuroscience and have spent many years programming, forming and testing hypotheses, and engineering interdisciplinary solutions to help people, research lines and organisations succeed. I thrive at the intersection of science, technology, and strategy. I have worked with all things data, from financial & cognitive modelling to machine learning approaches to image segmentation. I aim to continue to learn, grow and refine the way I approach problems, to help organisations make informed decisions and optimise their projects. As an investor, I’m particularly interested in supporting innovative companies that are pushing boundaries in biotech and medtech. Whether it’s advising on digital infrastructure or driving healthcare innovation, my goal is simple: to make a meaningful impact by supporting research and solutions that improve the way we live.
