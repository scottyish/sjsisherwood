About me

I’m a problem solver, investor, and passionate advocate for advancing life sciences and healthcare research. I hold a PhD in cognitive neuroscience and have spent many years behind a keyboard pursuing coding-heavy projects, I thrive at the intersection of science, technology, and strategy. I have worked with all things data, from financial modelling to machine learning in image segmentation, helping organisations make informed decisions and optimise their workflows. As an investor, I’m particularly interested in supporting innovative companies that are pushing boundaries in biotech and medtech. Whether it’s advising on digital infrastructure or driving healthcare innovation, my goal is simple: to make a meaningful impact by supporting research and solutions that improve the way we live.
